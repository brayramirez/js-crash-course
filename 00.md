## Basic Data Types
1. `number`
  - Integers and Decimal numbers.
  - Examples: `300`, `0`, `-75`, `3.22`

1. `string`
  - Can be enclosed with single `'` or double `"` quote.
  - Examples: `'Bacon'`, `"Cheese"`, `'Foo "Bar" Baz'`

1. `boolean`
  - Examples: `true`, `false`

1. `undefined`
  - Default value of a variable if nothing is assigned to it.
